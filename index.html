Bespoke luxury Italian travel, curated

Innamo curates bespoke luxury Italian travel, and takes its name from innamorata: the Italian word for a woman deeply, hopelessly in love. It describes our founder, Nesse, with some accuracy. Not in love with the idea of Italy, but with Italy itself: its light, its rhythm, its tables, its rooms. Every recommendation we make comes from Nesse directly, or from the small circle of trusted local contacts she has spent years cultivating. Every experience, carried home. Innamo exists to give you Italy the way she knows it. Not from a brochure. From the inside.
